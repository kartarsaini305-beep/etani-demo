Who We Are

More than a café —
it is a feeling.

Ètani Cafè is a women-owned, student-favourite hangout nestled right in front of IIIT Una. We brew specialty coffee, bake fresh pastries, and serve comforting meals without burning a hole in your pocket.

Whether you need a quiet corner to focus, a cozy nook to catch up with friends, or just a really good cup of coffee after a long lecture — we have always got you.
